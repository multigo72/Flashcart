FlashCart

Adds your list to an Ace Hardware cart for a store near your zip.

    Heads up: acehardware.com is behind Cloudflare bot protection. When the "Verify you are human" box appears in the browser window, tick it (and pick your local Ace if asked) — the bot detects this, waits, and continues on its own. Keep Watch the browser checked so you can do that. It fills the cart only — it never checks out or pays. Tune selectors in src/sites/ace.js if a step can't find something.
